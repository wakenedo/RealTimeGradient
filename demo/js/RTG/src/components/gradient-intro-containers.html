Gradient Containers
Gradient Containers let you bring interactive color flows to any part of your website. Think of them as living design elements — headers, cards, hero banners, or call-to-action blocks can now pulse with linear, radial, or text gradients that adapt in real time.

With just a few lines of setup, you can schedule gradients to shift with the time of day, trigger campaign-specific effects, or update designs at runtime. This makes them perfect for landing pages, product highlights, and marketing campaigns where visual impact drives customer conversion.

These are live examples of how containers can be styled — from smooth linear blends to dynamic radial bursts and even text-clipped gradients for typography that stands out.
